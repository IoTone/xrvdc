# XR VisionDevCamp

## Fukuoka Summer 2026

*Proposal v1.0.2*

![logo](/images/logo.png "logo")

---

## Proposal

Rebrand the VisionDevCamp hackathon as **"XR VisionDevCamp"** and run in Summer of 2026 in Fukuoka as a multi-site experience, culminating in final presentations judging at **CIC / Fukuoka One Building**.

Add training tracks to the front-end "pre-event" to ensure quality participation. Event should cover all XR devices.

---

## Proposal: Sites

- **Engineer Café** — Training / Hacking
- **CoWork Spaces** — Training / Hacking / Event at CIC (TBD)
- **Artist Café** — Public Art Space (?)

---

## Proposal: New AR Expo (TBD)

- Add an afternoon **AR Expo** before the event kicks off formally
- Free participation for vendors and projects to show off AR / VR / XR devices and services
- Promote local software developers and service providers
- Great way to build interest in the technology
- Tables like at a craft fair — nothing fancier unless companies bring it

---

## Proposal: Format

- Pre-event training at participant sites (week(s) before event)
- Demos at public spaces week(s) before event
- No formal live stream — encourage in-person participation (avoids gaming results); seek social media guru to post during event
- **Prizes** defined in advance, sponsored by participating companies
- **Devices** sponsored by XR companies
- Event = 2.5-day hackathon + VR Expo + afterparty

---

## Proposal: Dates

- **Between 6/26 – 6/28** ✅ *confirmed*
- Be careful of **WWDC**, **AWE**, and other Mac / iOS / XR events — seek proximity but not conflict
- Review calendars for possible event site participants

---

## Schedule

Ideally avoid competition with **AWE (June 15–18)** — [awexr.com/usa-2026](https://www.awexr.com/usa-2026/) — and **WWDC (June 6–12)**.

Recommended option: pre-events **6/12 - 6/21 ** (training, volunteer organizing, tech talks, unconf Expo); main event **6/26 – 6/28 **.

## Goals

**100 Participants in Fukuoka**

Breakout:

- 10 Teams
- 3 – 10 Mentors
- 10 Volunteers
- 30 Hackers
- 50-100 Attendees for final prizes (public, press, city officials)
- 1 – 2 Investors
- 1 – 5 follow-up *"business oriented"* results with clear post-event paths

*In 2025, the event drew about 90 participants in Tokyo and 30 in Fukuoka.*

## Value Proposition for Fukuoka

- Establish Fukuoka as a destination for **XR / AR / AI** events and developer talent
- Position the city-backed **Engineer Café** as a starting point for new ventures — moving beyond "nomad" work and freelancers
- Establish a clear path for participants to connect with industry opportunities — new ventures, collaborations, promotion
- Engage a broad audience: **Students, Makers, Artists, Hackers, Venture**
