Registration for **XR VisionDevCamp Fukuoka 2026** is officially open. The 2.5-day hackathon runs **Friday 26 June through Sunday 28 June** at **Engineer Café · CIC Fukuoka** — open to all XR / AR / VR-capable platforms and to all experience levels.

## Reserve your spot

Tickets are issued through Luma. Capacity is limited by the venue's footprint, so early registration is encouraged.

▸ [Register on Luma](https://luma.com/i5gerreb)

## What to expect

- **Friday 26 June** — 17:00 doors, keynote, mixer, late-night hacking
- **Saturday 27 June** — full hacking day
- **Sunday 28 June** — 12:00 submission cutoff, 13:00 judging, 16:00 awards, closing party

Source code and demo recordings are released publicly the evening of the closing ceremony.
