## XR VisionDevCamp 2026 — Event Overview

**Friday 26 June – Sunday 28 June, 2026** · Fukuoka, Japan.

A 2.5-day XR hackathon plus an AR Expo and pre-event training week, hosted across multiple sites in Fukuoka and culminating at **CIC Fukuoka / Fukuoka One Building**.

### Event Date

26 June (Fri) – 28 June (Sun), 2026

### Venue

- **Engineer Café — Hacker Space Fukuoka** (training, hacking floor)
- **CIC Fukuoka / Fukuoka One Building** (judging, finals)
- **CoWork Spaces** (additional training and hacking)

### Schedule (preliminary)

- **Fri 17:00** — Doors open / registration
- **Fri 18:00** — Keynote
- **Fri 19:00 – 24:00** — Mixer, team forming, in-person hacking
- **Sat** — Hacking + scheduled tech-talk presentations
- **Sun 12:00** — Submission cutoff (open-source preferred)
- **Sun 13:00 – 15:00** — Judging
- **Sun 16:00** — Award presentations
- **Sun evening** — Closing party (taco truck, outdoors)

### Devices

We are seeking sponsorship of **≥ 10 XR devices** for participants to use during the hackathon — ideally 2 per platform across:

- AVP M5
- Android XR (Samsung)
- New Xreal
- New Snap Spectacles
- New Meta Quest
- New HTC

### Prize Categories

Best Unity · Best AVP · Best Android XR · Best XR · Best Team · Best Solo · Best AI · Best Artistic · Best Concept · Best System Design · Best by Category (Game, Utility, Health & Fitness, EDU/Training, Storytelling).
