### A Historic Hackathon Originating from Silicon Valley

With its origins in the release of the iPhone in 2007, VisionDevCamp carries 18+ years of hackathon heritage.
Starting with **iPhoneDevCamp** (2007–2009), it expanded to **iPadDevCamp** (2010), **iOSDevCamp** (2010–2013), and now **VisionDevCamp** for the spatial-computing era.

### Event Format

Detailed format: [visiondevcamp.org/hackathon/](https://visiondevcamp.org/hackathon/)

What makes this hackathon distinct from larger events like MIT Reality Hack:

- **Collaboration over competition** — teams are encouraged to share code and knowledge.
- **No qualification gate** — open to all skill levels, with in-event learning sessions for beginners.
- **Flexible judging** — judges may invent new categories on the spot if a project deserves recognition.
- **Team size 1 – 6** — solo teams welcome; large teams discouraged.
- **Mixed-skill team formation** — staff actively support new participant team-up.
- **Multi-team participation** — you can join more than one team.
- **Public recognition** — winners receive social media validation and publicity.
- **Small monetary prizes** awarded after judging.
- **Loaner hardware** available to participants during the event.
